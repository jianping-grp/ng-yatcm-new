1. What is SEA?
SEA (Chemical similarity ensemble approach) has been approved as a promising tool and successfully applied in drug repositioning and natural products target prediction. In our previous work, we proposed a multi-voting SEA model for small molecule target prediction. Zhonghua by utilizing the multi-voting SEA model, all compounds in YaTCM can be systematically predicted and mapped to single protein targets from ChEMBL database. Four different molecular fingerprints (MACCS, Morgan, Topological and Atom pair) were used to build SEA models. 2. KEGG metabolic pathway mapping
The concept of metabolite-likeness, which derived from the fact that drugs are more structurally similar to endogenous metabolites than are library compounds, is a useful principle in drug design[51]. Kim et al.[4] reported that the degree of structural similarity between compounds derived from traditional oriental medicine and human metabolites is even higher than that of small molecule drug and human metabolites, and mapping of TCM compounds to human metabolic pathway based on structural similarity may helpful in analyses of mechanisms of actions[4]. Therefore, to make full use of ingredients data in YaTCM, all compounds were mapping to KEGG human metabolic pathway by calculating structural similarity of TCM-metabolite pairs. 3. Network building and analysis
In order to analyze different aspects of YaTCM data, we have developed a visualization interface by ECharts(http://echarts.baidu.com/) and Networkx(http://networkx.github.io/) from which the network can be displayed within webpage. 4.What is herb explorer?
Hu et al[52] apply a simple definition that two targets are related to each other if they share at least five active compounds. In our previous work, PhIN[53] database defined target-target interaction pairs in terms of shared compounds or shared scaffolds. As an analogy of this methodology, in YaTCM, to explorer the mechanism of action of herbs, one of two simple criteria, herb-target-herb criteria or herb-disease-herb, is used to defined an interacting herb pairs. Two herbs are defined as the same effect if they share a certain number of therapeutic targets or diseases. To give the user a global view of herb-*-herb interaction in database and conduct large-scale analysis, we also employ network visualization technology to display the network in herbsâ€™ detail pages where uses can choose the number of shared targets or diseases according the cursor. 5.What is prescription explorer?
TCM is characterized by the wide use of herbal formulae, which are capable of systematically treating diseases determined by interactions among various herbs. However, the molecule mechanism of action remains a mystery due to the lack of appropriate methods. So from a network and pathways prespective, we also build prescription explorer system. 6.
