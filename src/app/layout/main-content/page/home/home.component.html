Yet another Traditional Chinese Medicine

Database Introduction

The YaTCM database is a freely available resource that provides the comprehensive TCM information, including ingredients, herbs and prescriptions. YaTCM contains 47,696 natural compounds (including Chinese names, English names, PubChem CIDs, CAS numbers and structures), 6,220 herbs (including Chinese names, Latin names, medicinal parts, and Wikipedia links), 18,697 targets (3461 therapeutic targets included), 1,907 predicted targets, 390 pathways and 1813 prescriptions. The database provides keyword (herb, formulae, structure, English name, Chinese name, plant name, CID, CAS) search, similarity search and substructure search. In addition, The YaTCM website is not only a data repository. It also furnished with tools for query, visualization and analysis of TCM result on the systematic network level that can present their connection to facilitate the understanding of mechanism of actions of TCM and research of combination therapy. The purpose of YaTCM is to help TCM researchers increase efficiency in TCM moderation process. YaTCM would serve as a valuable data resource in TCM-derived drug discovery
